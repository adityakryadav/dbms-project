Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can I speak to a doctor?

Most consultations start within 10–15 minutes depending on doctor availability. You’ll receive a confirmation as soon as a doctor is assigned.

Are my consultations confidential?

Yes. All chats and calls are encrypted and confidential. Only you and your assigned doctor can access your consultation details.

Can I get a prescription after the consultation?

In most cases, yes. If the doctor deems it medically appropriate, a digital prescription will be provided after your consultation.

What specialities are available?

We cover General Medicine, Pediatrics, Dermatology, Gynecology, ENT, Psychiatry, and more. Your query is routed to the right specialist.

What if it’s an emergency?

Online consultations are not for emergencies. Please call local emergency services or visit the nearest hospital immediately.
